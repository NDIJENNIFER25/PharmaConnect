About the Project

PharmaConnect is a smart, user-friendly platform that helps people find nearby pharmacies with the drugs they need—fast. Built by a passionate student team, we’re focused on making healthcare more accessible through real-time search, geolocation, and collaboration between customers and pharmacies.
